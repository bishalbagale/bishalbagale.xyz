she is beautiful

Posted on Oct 23, 2023

she is beautiful
by the books
and the songs
about love

she is beautiful
like the twilight
sun and a
poet’s muse.

she is beautiful
so sings the
river, lullaby to
the clouds.

she is beautiful
like a mother,
with loving heart
and boundless grace.

she is beautiful
like the life
itself.

she is beautiful
and she doesn’t
know.
